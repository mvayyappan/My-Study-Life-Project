SSC CGL

The Staff Selection Commission - Combined Graduate Level Examination, often referred to as SSC CGL is an examination to recruit staff to various posts in ministries, departments and organisations of the Government of India.

Syllabus

The exam comprises of two Tiers, Tier-I and Tier-II containing Quantitative Aptitude, English, Reasoning, and General Awareness.

Syllabus

The syllabus includes Numerical and Mathematical Ability, Reasoning Ability and Problem Solving, General Awareness, and English Language.

Central Police Organization

SSC CPO

SSC CPO is an open competitive examination for recruitment to the posts of Sub-Inspector (SI) in Delhi Police and Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs).

Syllabus

The exam consists of Paper-I, Physical Standard Test (PST)/ Physical Endurance Test (PET), Paper-II, and Detailed Medical Examination (DME).
