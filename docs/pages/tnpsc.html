TNPSC Group III

The TNPSC Group III exam is held for the recruitment of candidates at the post of Senior Accountant, Auditor, Assistant Section Officer, etc.

Syllabus

For a General candidate, the minimum age limit to apply for the TNPSC Group 3 exams is 18, and the maximum is 42 as per the last recruitment pattern. To be eligible for a TNPSC Group-3 post, a candidate must have a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized University with the specialization relevant to the post he/she is applying for as per the last recruitment post

The selection process of the TNPSC Group 3 exam includes the written test and interview test. In the written test, the aspirants have to answer both the objective and descriptive type questions. The shortlisted aspirants from the written test will be called for the interview round. Once the final selection process will be done, the candidates will be gone through the document verification process.
